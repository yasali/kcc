Kista Cricket Club (KCC) is the section for cricket at Kista Sports Club. It manages all the cricketing matters at Kista Sports Club. KCC trying to promote cricket in Sweden by participating in various tournaments organized by the Swedish Cricket Federation (Swedish Cricket Federation). In addition, KCC organizes activities for children to participate in order to learn how to play cricket. For more information about the KCC, you can find the KCC website http://www.kistacc.se/
